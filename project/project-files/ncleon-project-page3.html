VOTE! The importance of voting

So, why is voting so important?

Everyone knows that voting is one of our many civil duties, but what exactly does that mean? Why is it so important to go out and vote?
Here is a list of 5 reasons to vote in the upcoming election.

  1. You have a say in the life you want for yourself and future generations
  2. By not voting, you are giving your power away
  3. You have a chance to choose where your tax dollars are going
  4. Voting is a major way to encourange positive change
  5. Your community depends on you! Not everyone is fortunate enough to be able to vote.
