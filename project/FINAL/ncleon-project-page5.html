You may request an absentee ballot if you expect to be:
  • Away from your designated county on Election Day.
  • Unable to appear at the polls on Election Day due to illness, injury or physical disability, or you are a primary care giver to an ill, injured, or physically disabled individual(s).
  • Detained in jail awaiting Grand Jury action or confined in prison after conviction for a crime other than a felony.
