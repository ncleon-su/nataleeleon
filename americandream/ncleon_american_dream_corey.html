Corey Schwaitzberg biography

Corey Schwaitzberg is a 24 year old male of European descent born about 20 miles south of Boston in Canton, Massachusetts. He grew up in a wealthy suburban neighborhood with his parents and brother. As a child, Corey spent a lot of time with his grandmother who has greatly impacted the way in which he approaches life. He went on to earn a degree in communication studies and philosophy with a minor in legal studies from Northwestern University.

Today, Corey is living in Boston and is applying to master's programs in digital media studies. He does marketing, communications, and operations for Brain Power - a startup company that writes software for Google Glass to help teach behavioral skills to children with autism. He looks forward to exploring different career paths in the future.
